WHO WE ARE

Empowering Communities, One Wing at a Time

Founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic by passionate young students, NayePankh Foundation has grown into one of India's largest student-run NGOs. We believe every individual deserves equal access to opportunities, dignity, and growth.

Our name "NayePankh" means "New Wings" in Hindi — symbolizing our mission to give wings to those who need them most. Through education, healthcare, environment, and community initiatives, we're building a brighter India.
