Our Identity

Wings of Change

NayePankh Foundation ("New Wings") is one of India's leading youth-driven NGOs, working relentlessly to empower the underprivileged and give wings to every dream.

Our Incredible Journey

A look back at how a small youth collective grew to impact thousands of lives across the country.

2020

The Seed is Sown

Founded by a group of passionate students during the global pandemic, NayePankh began its operations with a single food distribution drive in Kanpur, UP, helping daily wage workers survive lockdowns.

2021

Expanding Horizons

Recognizing education as the primary tool for long-term empowerment, NayePankh launched its flagship education drives, providing free literacy and stationery kits to children living in slum areas.

2022

Health & Sanitation

We organized rural health check-up camps, distributed sanitary pads to slum women, and conducted awareness workshops on personal hygiene and nutritional health.

2023

10,000+ Volunteers Strong

With a growing digital presence, NayePankh expanded nationwide. Over 10,000 registered volunteers joined our campaigns, reaching multiple states and urban centres.

2024

Smart Digital Volunteer Nexus

Empowering our volunteers with technology. We launched the Volunteer Nexus platform featuring 3D interactions, predictive analytics, and conversational AI helper agents.
