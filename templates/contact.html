Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Find resolutions to the most common queries below.

What is the minimum age to volunteer? ▼

Anyone who is 15 years or older can register as a volunteer with NayePankh Foundation. For students under 18, school approval or parental consent might be requested during specific community drives.

Do volunteers get certificates? ▼

Yes, all active volunteers receive a Certificate of Volunteering, appreciation letters, and direct performance reviews from the leadership team based on their total hours contributed and campaign attendance.

Can I volunteer remotely? ▼

Yes! We have dedicated remote roles in Social Media Marketing, Graphic Design, Content Writing, and Web Development. Make sure to specify your digital skills during registration so we can match you appropriately.

How does the retention prediction score work? ▼

Our platform uses a Random Forest Classifier trained on historical volunteering patterns (age, skills count, location, availability) to estimate engagement likelihood. This helps our HR team support volunteers at risk of dropping out and assign optimal mentor groups.

How are donation funds spent? ▼

Over 90% of all public contributions go directly towards purchasing food, educational kits, healthcare equipment, and managing skill-empowerment hubs. Admin audits and transparency charts are visible in our annual reports.
