Flagship Program

Shiksha Path (Shiksha Abhiyaan)

At NayePankh, we believe that education is the fundamental ticket to breaking generational poverty. Under our 'Shiksha Path' campaign, we set up community classrooms, distribute stationery kits, and sponsor school admissions for children living in slum areas and rural areas.
